Understanding Your Results

This advanced calculator provides comprehensive insights about your body composition and nutritional needs.

BMI Categories:

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5-24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25-29.9
  • Obese: BMI 30 or greater

About BMR

Basal Metabolic Rate is the number of calories your body needs at complete rest to maintain basic functions like breathing and circulation.

Activity Levels

Your daily calorie needs are calculated based on your activity level using the Harris-Benedict equation for accurate results.
